About The Label

Riqos Productions is built on one core principle: execution over ideas. We don’t just create music or visuals — we shape direction, identity, and long-term creative positioning. Every project is treated like a release, not a task.

To become a leading independent creative label shaping new-generation sound and visual culture.
Mission
Turn raw artistic potential into industry-ready output across music and film.
Approach
Minimal ego, maximum execution. Every detail matters — sound, visuals, branding.
